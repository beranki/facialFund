Step 2: Camera Feed


This is the camera feed that registers your biometric data.

Make sure you follow these steps:

  • Make sure there is only ONE recognizable face in frame so that it doesn't pollute the data.
  • Try doing different poses and moving your face around in different ways during this stage -
    this is important so the data has different distributions.
  • As well as these two steps, refer to the bounding box on the camera feed and confidence percentage to correspondingly adjust the lighting or position for ideal conditions.
  • When you pause the video stream, it no longer registers photos. As a helpful indicator, doing so will temporarily remove the camera feed from the webpage until you unpause it.

  • However, after a certain period of time, the camera feed will pause meaning the needed samples have been reached. At this point, proceed to the user menu. You have fully completed the registration!
